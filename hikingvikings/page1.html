Island of Iceland

If you’re wondering whether Iceland is just an unpredictably cold-weathered island with ice, then you’re right. But it’s much more than that, don’t let the cold deter you from seeing what magical, beautiful and captivating country Iceland really is. Lets go on a hike to Iceland.

This site is for those who wanderlust more than a sandy beach with a cocktail in their hands. Not to say that anything is wrong with that, but there’s beauty that’s meant to be seen, outside of your comfort vacation.
