How to make raw vegan blueberry muffins



    INGREDIENTS

  1. 2 ½ cup whole grain rolled oats; gluten-free

  2. 1 cup raw brazil nuts

  3. 1/2 cup flax seed; ground

  4. 2 bananas; mashed

  5. 1 cup dried blueberries; soaked for 1 hour

  6. 1/3 cup agave nectar -or- coconut nectar

  7. 1/4 cup coconut palm sugar

  8. 1 tbsp cinnamon

  9. 1 tsp vanilla extract; or ½ vanilla bean

  10. 1 tsp salt

INSTRUCTIONS

  • In a food processor, process rolled oats into a flour.

  • Add in brazil nuts and process again.

  • In a spice grinder, grind flax seed.

  • In a large mixing bowl mash bananas.

  • Add in the rest of the ingredients to the mixing bowl and mix well.p

  • Add your mixture to muffin cups in a muffin tray and dehydrate at 165 degrees for 1-2 hours, turn down to 105 degrees for 5-6 hours.
